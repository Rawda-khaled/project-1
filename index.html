The process


Simply click through the slide images at each step to see the designs in picture format as this will help you to create your own beautiful cake. After you have selected all the required fields, the final cost will add up at the bottom of the page and you can complete the cake order by making the payment online.

Delivery


We value your convenience and helping you to celebrate the special day with ease! So, if you decide to have your cake delivered, please provide the address and pick the delivery time. Cakes are very fragile items so we are taking utmost care with each and every cake to make sure that it arrives to you safely.

Ingredients


All our ingredients are sourced locally and only the highest quality ingredients are used at each process from the cake base to our buttercream. As a cake company, we really value where our ingredients come from and how we can create a positive impact on the society as much as possible.
